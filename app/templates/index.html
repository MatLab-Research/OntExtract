Semantic Change Analysis Framework

Context Anchors

Define semantic neighborhoods of terms fixed to specific meanings at particular points in time for drift measurement.

Temporal Analysis

Track semantic drift through neighborhood overlap, positional change, and similarity reduction across time periods.

Fuzziness Scoring

Measure degree of membership retention in original semantic categories using continuous rather than binary classifications.

Uncertainty Management

Handle interpretive ambiguity through provisional classifications and confidence-based weights for borderline cases.

PROV-O Provenance

Document complete audit trails using W3C Provenance Ontology for traceable semantic change detection.

Version Control

Maintain branching semantic histories with parallel meaning trajectories and convergence tracking over time.
