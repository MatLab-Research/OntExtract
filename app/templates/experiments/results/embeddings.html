About Embeddings
What are Embeddings?

Embeddings are dense vector representations of text that capture semantic meaning. Similar texts have similar embeddings, enabling semantic search and comparison.

Embedding Methods
  • local: Sentence transformers (fast, local)
  • openai: OpenAI text-embedding-3-small
  • period_aware: Historical language models
Usage

Embeddings enable semantic similarity search, document clustering, and cross-temporal comparison of concept meanings.
